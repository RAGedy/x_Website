FDE Intern — Summer 2026

San Francisco / New York · Hybrid or remote · 10–12 weeks

The role

Spend the summer deploying Ember alongside our Founding FDE at one of our design-partner firms. You'll sit in a PE or consulting client's office, map their document corpus, ship the integration scripts, and help build the eval harness that decides whether a deployment is working. End-to-end retrieval work that almost no other internship offers.

You are

  • An undergrad or grad student who writes Python comfortably — CS, math, physics, engineering, or a finance / consulting student who codes seriously on the side.
  • Curious about retrieval, knowledge graphs, and the parts of finance and consulting where information is the moat.
  • Willing to read documents nobody else wants to read.

How to apply

Send a one-page note to founders@allember.com with the subject "FDE Intern — Summer 2026." Tell us about one technical project you shipped end-to-end, what you'd want to learn from a summer at Ember, and your earliest start / latest end dates.

Rolling review until filled.

Ember Intelligence is an equal opportunity employer. We hire on the basis of what you can build, what you have built, and how you think — full stop.
